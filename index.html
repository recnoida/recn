RECN NOIDA

We love to ride!

Welcome to the Royal Enfield Club Noida, a community of passionate riders who share a love for Royal Enfield motorcycles. Established in 2014, our club is dedicated to promoting safe and responsible riding while providing a platform for riders of all skill levels to connect and enjoy the thrill of the open road together.


Admin


Ishaan Sharma

Meet Ishaan Sharma, the founder and administrator of the Royal Enfield Club Noida. A passionate rider and Royal Enfield enthusiast, Ishaan established the club in 2014 with a vision of bringing together like-minded riders and promoting safe and responsible riding.


As the club's administrator, Ishaan is dedicated to creating a welcoming and supportive community for riders of all skill levels. He is committed to organizing engaging events, training sessions, and workshops that offer members the opportunity to improve their riding skills and connect with other enthusiasts.

Admin


Anmol Mathur

Meet Anmol Mathur, co-founder of the Royal Enfield Club Noida and a passionate off-road enthusiast. With his love for adventure and the thrill of the open road, Anmol brings a unique perspective to the club's vision of promoting safe and responsible riding.


As the club's co-founder, Anmol has played a key role in developing the club's community and value system. Anmol's expertise in off-road riding has also helped the club establish itself as a hub for adventure-seeking riders.

Admin


Lesslie A.k.a Lezz

Meet Leslie Robin, co-founder of the Royal Enfield Club Noida and a driving force behind the club's values and organization. Leslie's passion for riding and dedication to promoting safe and responsible riding has helped shape the club into the community it is today.


As a co-founder, Leslie has played a key role in establishing the club's values of safety, respect, and community. Leslie's commitment to these values has also led to the creation of a dedicated team of marshals who manage convoy discipline on every ride, ensuring that all members ride safely and responsibly.
