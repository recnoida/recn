RECN NOIDA

We love to ride!

We are a group of passionate people coming from diversed background and expertise but what brings us together is the common interest to own a royal enfield and ride it together in the brotherhood. With a basic and strong moto which was laid down back then and is religously being followed till now
"Live Strong Ride Strong".

So this group was established around 8 years back with couple of passionate riders sharing common passion for biking and having similar defination for adveture and learning
"You can upgrade for any seat modification but it would be still pain in your ass".


Admin


Ishaan Sharma

The core idea behind riding a bike and starting a community is his brain child. Ishaan started riding his RE as a teenager and he fondly shares his story of how his riding journey started with the Royal Enfield bike that was owned by his father. Chirpy nature, extreme passion for bike riding and commendable road sense with a brilliant aptitude for taking care of any and everything about the group has literally compelled other riders to follow his foot steps. He now owns a RE classic and an RE Interceptor and has covered most of the North India on his bikes.

Admin


Anmol Mathur

Co-founder of the RECN group, Anmol is too passionate about riding his bike on every type of terrain, be it plain highways, hilly curves, sand/dirt, slush, rough terrains and what not. Looks to be a quiet and introvert person at first glimpse, but opens up his heart and gets difficult to stop once he starts talking about Off-roading, RE bikes or the RECN community. Part of the early formation days of RECN, Anmol proudly shares about the group’s disciplinary rules and the most important philosophy of RECN - “We go together and we come back together. We do not leave any brother behind or alone.”. Anmol owns a RE Thunderbird 500, which he likes to call as “Beast”.

Admin


Lesslie A.k.a Lezz

Big brother of the group, Lezz is the one who taught the basic fundamentals a rider must follow while riding on their bikes, dealing with volatile road conditions, unpredictable road users, tough weather, etc. He is also the Lead Marshal of the group who plays a vital role in ensuring that all the team members reach back home safely. Strict but equally loving, Lezz has been respected by everyone in the community for his helpful nature. He rides a RE Thunderbird X 500 and has been riding bikes since 2 decades.
